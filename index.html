About the Film

"Ooru Peru Akhandasthalam" is a haunting supernatural thriller set in a cursed village plagued by caste-based atrocities. The story follows a group of friends who unknowingly awaken a restless spirit named Rudra — a child burned alive for breaking caste boundaries. As each episode unfolds, eerie visions, ritualistic hauntings, and suppressed village secrets emerge, revealing Rudra’s chilling backstory and his vengeance. On every Amavasya night, he returns with a message: justice delayed is justice delivered in blood. Rooted in folklore, this series tackles dark truths about caste, guilt, and revenge.
